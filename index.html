I love Avocados

Also known as an alligator pear or butter fruit, avocados are actually a type of berry. They grow in warm climates. Avocados contain a wide range of nutrients and may have various health benefits. Soft avocados, for example, make delicious guacamole, while firmer avocados are great for slicing and adding to a salad or sandwich. People can use avocados in many other forms, such as an oil for cooking or moisturizing the skin or hair.
